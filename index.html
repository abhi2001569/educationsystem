* Rule and Regulation

  1. Respectful Communication Always communicate respectfully with fellow members. Personal attacks, harassment, and offensive language are strictly prohibited.
  2. No Spamming Refrain from posting spam, advertisements, or promotional content not related to educational purposes.
  3. Academic Integrity Do not engage in cheating, plagiarism, or any form of academic dishonesty. Respect the intellectual property of others.
  4. Privacy Protection Respect the privacy of others. Do not share personal information or private conversations without consent.
  5. Constructive Feedback Provide constructive and respectful feedback. Avoid derogatory or demeaning comments.
  6. Appropriate Content Ensure all content shared is appropriate for an educational environment. Explicit or inappropriate material is not allowed.
  7. Use of Forums Post in the appropriate forums and threads. Stay on topic and contribute meaningfully to discussions.
  8. Study Group Conduct Collaborate effectively and respectfully in study groups. Contribute fairly and support your peers.
  9. Event Participation Participate in events and webinars responsibly. Respect the organizers, presenters, and fellow attendees.
  10. Report Violations Report any violations of these rules to the moderators or administrators. Help maintain a positive and supportive community environment.
